Our streamlined application process allows you to apply for projects and receive approvals quickly. Plus, we provide resources and support to help you successfully complete your projects and fulfill your Eagle Scout requirements. Use our website to focus on making a meaningful impact and reaching your scouting goals.

Getting Started

Scouts

Getting started with our platform is simple and hassle-free. You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information. We respect your privacy and do not collect any data. All you need to do is browse through the list of available projects and find one that interests you. Each project listing includes contact information for the organization, allowing you to reach out directly. All communication and arrangements are handled externally, between you and the organization. We provide you with the contact details, and the rest is up to you.

Organizations

To start using our platform, you'll need to apply to create an account. We keep the application process straightforward by only asking for your organization's name, address, and a contact email. Once you submit your application, our team will manually verify the details to ensure legitimacy. Upon approval, you will receive an email confirming your account and providing login details. After logging in, you can begin posting your projects right away. This process ensures that only reputable organizations are part of our community, providing a trustworthy environment for Eagle Scouts to find meaningful projects.
