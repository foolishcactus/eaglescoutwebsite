Eagle Scout Project Match

Connecting Eagle Scouts with meaningful projects from reputable organizations, helping Scouts achieve their ranks while organizations benefit from dedicated support.

For Scouts

Are you finding it difficult to locate suitable projects to achieve your Eagle Scout rank? Facing difficulties in connecting with organizations that have meaningful projects available?

Our platform simplifies your search for Eagle Scout projects. With a wide range of projects from reputable organizations, you can easily find opportunities that match your interests and skills. Our streamlined application process allows you to apply for projects and receive approvals quickly. Plus, we provide resources and support to help you successfully complete your projects and fulfill your Eagle Scout requirements. Use our website to focus on making a meaningful impact and reaching your scouting goals.

For Organizations

Struggling to find reliable volunteers for your important projects? Limited outreach and resource constraints to attract dedicated individuals who can help achieve your goals? Difficulty Managing and coordinating volunteer efforts?

Our platform connects you with motivated Eagle Scouts eager to take on meaningful projects. By posting your projects on our site, you gain access to a pool of dedicated scouts committed to making a positive impact. Our user-friendly interface makes it easy to post, manage, and track projects, ensuring a streamlined process. With our platform, you can overcome volunteer shortages and focus on fulfilling your mission with the support of enthusiastic and capable scouts.

Getting Started

Scouts

Getting started is easy. No account or personal information is needed; we respect your privacy. Simply browse available projects and find one that interests you. Each listing includes the organization's contact info for direct communication. All arrangements are handled between you and the organization. We provide the contact details, and you handle the rest.

Organizations

To start using our platform, simply apply to create an account. We only ask for your organization's name, address, and contact email. After submitting your application, our team will manually verify the details. Once approved, you will receive an email with your login information. After logging in, you can start posting your projects immediately. This ensures that only reputable organizations are part of our community, providing a trustworthy environment for Eagle Scouts to find meaningful projects.
