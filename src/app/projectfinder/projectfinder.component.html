Project Finder

What is the Project Finder?

Here local organizations have posted projects that they have deemed within the scope of an Eagle Project. The goal is to improve the community by bridging the gap between non-profit organizations that need resources and prospective Eagle Scouts who need to earn their ranks. A win-win situation where both benefit.

As a Scout, How should I use this?

This will simplify the process of picking your project. These are not final projects, but simply a starting guide for a particular idea and organization. Your next steps will be to reach out as the Scout and move further along the process. All we have done here is make it easier finding the organizations.

As an Organization, How should I use this?

If you are a non-profit organization and are need of small projects being done, then contact us directly or please fill out the form to get your project on the website. We will not be handling communication between the organization and the Scout. After posting on this website, all communication falls between the Scout and the Organization. After confirmation of the project please contact us to remove your posting from our site.
